About Us

Learn more about the International Crane Foundation and our mission

Our Mission

The International Crane Foundation (ICF) is a non-profit conservation organization established in 1973 and registered in the USA, headquartered in Baraboo, Wisconsin. We work worldwide to conserve cranes and the ecosystems, watersheds, and flyways on which they depend.

Our Conservation Efforts

We are committed to providing knowledge, leadership, and inspiration to resolve threats to cranes and their diverse landscapes.
